The above-mentioned functions model devices with 0V as logical low-level and 1V as logical high-level. Of course, they can be easily transformed into higher voltage levels by multiplying the desired high-level voltage to the output voltage and dividing the input voltages by the desired high-level voltage. Note: The derivatives also get divided by the desired high-level voltage, but they are not multiplied by the desired high-level voltage.
